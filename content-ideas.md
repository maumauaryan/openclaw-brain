## The Viral Discovery Method

### Step 1 — Strategic Search
**Find 50K-500K follower accounts**
- Go to Instagram → Search bar
- Type PROBLEM/DESIRE keywords (not industry terms)
- Fitness: "lose belly fat" (not "fitness")
- Business: "make money online" (not "entrepreneurship")

### Step 2 — Outlier Identification
**Find 5–10x performance relative to average**

**Tool:** SortFeed Chrome extension
- Scans last 50-100 videos
- Sorts by views instantly
- Finds outliers in seconds

**:warning: Don't look at raw view counts**
- Average = 20K
- 200K video = 10x outlier = GOLD
- 50K video from 20K average = NOT outlier

### Step 3 — Hook Deconstruction
**Write down ALL three elements**

1. **Spoken Hook** — First 3-5 seconds (word for word)
2. **Visual Hook** — What happens on screen
3. **Text Hook** — Words written on screen

**Store in:** Notion database → patterns emerge over time

### Step 4 — Ethical Adaptation
**Keep format, change value**

Like pasta format → use your recipe:
- Keep exact hook structure
- Change value completely (your expertise)
- Not copying — using proven packaging

## Content Ideation Pyramid

### Level 1 — Highest Probability (80% of content)
**Viral Video in Your Niche**
→ Same Hook + Same Format + Your Value

**Most reliable.** Learn from what works.

---

### Level 2 — Very High Probability
**Your Own Best Content → Repurposed**
→ Same topic, same format, same hook (different version)

**The Netflix Principle:** Don't create pilots. Create Season 2.

---

### Level 3 — Good Probability
**Viral Video in Adjacent Niche**
→ Adapt Hook + Your Niche Format

**Example:** Marketing concepts applied to fitness

---

### Level 4 — Experimental (20%)
**Different Niche → Same Format Only**
→ Borrow format, use your topic

**Best for:** Testing creative risk

## Idea Sources

### Competitor Comments 📝
Go to top creator → Read last 3 posts' comments
- What are people asking?
- What confusion exists?
- Every question = potential post

**Time:** 15 min → 5+ ideas

### Your Own DMs 📩
Every question from someone is proof others have it
- "How do you..." → reel
- "I struggle with..." → carousel
- Pushback → contrarian post

**Store in:** "Questions from DMs" Notion page

### Reddit Threads 💬
r/Entrepreneur, r/marketing, r/smallbusiness
- Top posts = proven pain points
- Vent posts = exact client pain
- Questions with 100+ upvotes = high demand

**Use their exact words** as hooks

### Your Own Week 📅
Journal 3 lines daily: "What did I notice?"
- "I noticed..." → Authority post
- "I made a mistake..." → Personal post
- "How I did..." → Process post

**Best content = what you actually experienced.**

### Viral Content Deconstruction 🔍
Find 3 high-performing posts per week
Analyze: hook type, pillar, CTA, format
Extract **PRINCIPLE**, not execution

**Never copy. Always ask "what's the underlying mechanic?"**
