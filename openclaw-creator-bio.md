## About OpenClaw (Official Website: `openclaw.ai`)

**Identity:**
"The AI that actually does things. Your personal assistant on any platform."

**Capabilities:**
- Clears inbox, sends emails, manages calendar
- Checks in for flights
- Operates via WhatsApp, Telegram, and any chat app
- Persistent memory, persona onboarding, 24/7 automation
- Skills system (extensible workflows), heartbeats (reminders), background tasks

**Philosophy:**
Context and skills live on **your computer**, not a walled garden.
Open source. Growing community building skills.

**Technical Vasibility:**
- Runs on any OS (Linux, macOS, Windows)
- Integrates with: Gmail, Calendar, WordPress, Hetzner
- Can control: WHOOP, home automation (Winix air purifiers), flight check-in
- Proactive background agent: cron jobs, reminders, task scheduling
- 24/7 assistant with persistent context
