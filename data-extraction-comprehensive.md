## Core Agency Vision (EVOLUTION)

## Core Philosophy & Beliefs

"A talker doesn't run a family. A doer does."

**Key beliefs:**
- Freelancing is time-for-money → no leverage
- High-income agencies sell: ROI, Multiplication, Systems
- Editing skill alone ≠ money
- Positioning + virality + brand systems = money

**Rich clients:**
- Care less about small ROI fluctuations
- Think long-term
- Have better networks
- Refer other rich people

**Poorer clients:**
- Micromanage
- Demand instant ROI
- Stress-heavy

---

## Target Clients (EVOLUTION)

### Initially
Creators making $5K–$15K/month
Small creators stuck in bottlenecks

### Later (REJECTED)
$3K–$5K retainers, many clients, high stress, low leverage

### Final Target
**High-ticket only**
- People making: $4M+, $5M, $10M+, $12M+, $20M+ per year
- Examples: Entrepreneurs, Founders, Investors, Coaches, Course creators
- Especially: People already creating content but not optimized, not viral, not positioned well

---

## Pricing Strategy (ALL VERSIONS)

### Early Pricing
$1.5K–$3K/month (starter), $5K–$10K/month (mid-tier)

### High-Ticket Direction
**Minimum:** $10K/month
**Preferred:** $15K–$20K/month
**Scaling:** $25K/month, $40K/month

### Dynamic Pricing Model (FINAL)
- Retainer based on client revenue
- Client making $5M/year → ~$20K/month
- Client making $20M/year → ~$40K/month
- Annual cost framed as ~5% or less of enterprise value growth
