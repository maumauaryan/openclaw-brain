## When to Trigger

When Aryan says "draft DM for [name]" or after lead research with verdict "Worth outreach"

## DM Rules

- **Open with:** a specific observation about their content or business
- **Banned phrases:** "I love your content", "I help founders like you", "Would love to hop on a call"
- **Tone:** peer-to-peer, calm, observational
- **Length:** 3-5 sentences MAX
- **End with:** natural soft observation or question. NOT a CTA.
- **Never mention:** the agency, services, or pricing

## Framework

1. **Specific observation** (what you actually noticed)
2. **What that signals** (insight they might not have articulated)
3. **Soft natural bridge** (not a pitch)
