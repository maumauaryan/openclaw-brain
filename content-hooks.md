## 6 Hook Archetypes (CCC Framework)

### 01 — Mute Hooks ⭐ Most Powerful
**Rule:** No language needed — pure visual story

**Why it works:**
- Removes biggest barrier to consumption
- Global reach (no translation needed)

**Examples:**
- 100 views vs 1M views split screen
- Camera equipment comparison
- Before/after editing

**Best for:** Maximum reach, going global

---

### 02 — Strong Polarizing Takes
**Rule:** Trigger emotion with contrarian claim

**Formula:** `[Strong claim] + [counterintuitive element]`

**Examples:**
- "What used to work on Instagram is dead"
- "Stop posting every day"
- "You're ruining your content if..."

**Best for:** High engagement, algorithm loves controversy

---

### 03 — Quick Tips
**Rule:** Immediately applicable + numbered steps

**Formula:** `Number + specific action + clear outcome`

**Examples:**
- "These 3 Instagram settings get you more views"
- "One change doubled my sales"
- "Do this before you post any content"

**Best for:** High saves, technical niches

---

### 04 — Starting From Scratch
**Rule:** Founder returns to zero — documents journey

**Formula:** "If I had to start [again/from scratch/from zero]..."

**Examples:**
- "If I lost everything and had to rebuild"
- "Starting from 0 followers, I'd do this"

**Best for:** Beginner magnets, high relatability

---

### 05 — Reactions & Breakdowns
**Rule:** Borrow authority + viral content

**Examples:**
- React to Alex Hormozi clip
- Break down Iman Gadzhi's brand
- Analyze Mr. Beast strategy

**Best for:** Beginning, borrow virality from established creators

---

### 06 — Split Screen / Duality
**Rule:** Show contrast, create dopamine spikes

**Examples:**
- Good hook vs bad hook
- Cheap vs expensive setup
- AI on vs AI off

**Best for:** Visual comparison, combine with mute hooks

---

## Hook Writing Rules

### The 3-Second Rule
**If it doesn't stop the scroll in first 3 seconds — it's dead.**

### Visual + Spoken = Maximum Capture
- 85% of people watch on mute
- Need BOTH visual and spoken hooks
- Overlay text on screen for mute viewers

### Test Matrix
Rotate through all 6 types over 2 weeks
Never use same type twice in a row
Track which type gets highest watch time → double down
