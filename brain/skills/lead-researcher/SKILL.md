## When to Trigger

When Aryan says "research [name]" or "qualify [URL]"

## Steps

1. Find their Instagram/YouTube/LinkedIn
2. Analyze: what do they post about? Business model? Revenue signals?
3. Check content quality: poor hooks? weak positioning? inconsistent posting?
4. Check audience and engagement quality
5. Score 1-10 based on: revenue signals + content gap + ICP match (US/UK/CA/AU)

## Output Format

---
**Lead:** [name] | [handle]
**Business:** [what they do, rough revenue signal]
**Content gap:** [what's wrong / underperforming]
**ICP score:** [X/10]
**Best angle:** [1 specific observation to open DM with]
**Verdict:** Research more | Worth outreach | Skip
---

## Notes

- Spend 5-10 minutes max per lead
- Revenue signals: team size, office location, pricing page mentions, podcasts/interviews
- Content gap look for: no clear POV, inconsistent posting, talking to wrong audience
