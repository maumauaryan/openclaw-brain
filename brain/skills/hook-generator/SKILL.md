## When to Trigger

"generate hooks for [topic]" or during midday check

## 8 Hook Types

Generate one of each, pick best 5:

1. **Contrarian:** "Most people think X. They're wrong."
2. **Curiosity gap:** "Here's what nobody tells you about X"
3. **Bold claim:** "X changed everything. Here's why."
4. **Question:** "What if [uncomfortable truth]?"
5. **Story:** "I [lost/gained X]. Then I discovered [Y]."
6. **Number:** "3 things that got me [result] without [sacrifice]"
7. **Warning:** "Stop [common thing]. Here's what to do instead."
8. **Observation:** "I've worked with 50+ founders. The pattern is always the same."

## Brand Filter

All hooks must align with:
- Clarity, restraint, authority, founder psychology

**Reject:**
- Hustle language ("grind", "hustle", "crush it")
- Fake urgency ("act now", "before it's too late")
- Hype, bro-marketing, dopamine triggers

## Output

5 hooks, ranked by predicted performance:
```
[Hook] | Type: [X] | Why it works: [one line]
```
