## 5-Part CCC Viral Script Structure

### 1 — HOOK ⚡
**Capture attention in 0–3 seconds**

**Rules:**
- Take hook from proven viral video in niche
- DO NOT invent your own — leverage what works
- Include BOTH spoken and visual hook

**Capture:** Spoken words + On-screen action + Text overlay

---

### 2 — SELLING THE SOLUTION 💎
**Create desire without giving value**

**5 Methods:**
- **Social Proof:** "These are the same principles we used to get to 460K followers"
- **FOMO:** "If you're not applying this, you'll never get more than 200 views"
- **Hype:** "These are by far the most important things for growth"
- **Uniqueness:** "That no one talks about"
- **Pre-handle Objections:** "And no, I'm NOT just going to tell you to post more"

**Test:** Can you remove this and still make sense?

---

### 3 — GIVING THE PRINCIPLE 📖
**The actual concept**

**Rules:**
- Explain clearly (6th grade level)
- If you can say it with fewer words — DO SO
- Value per minute is everything

**Density Rule:** More value per second = more rewatches

---

### 4 — MAKING IT APPLICABLE ✅
**Optional but powerful**

**3 Methods:**
- Show it on screen / demo
- Give actionable next steps
- Link to deeper resource

**When to use:**
- Technical content requires this
- Results-focused posts need this
- Skip for pure mindset/authority posts

---

### 5 — CALL TO ACTION 🎯
**One action only**

**Examples:**
- "Comment 'SYSTEM' and I'll send you..."
- "Follow for the breakdown this week"
- "Share this with a founder who needs it"

**Rule:** One action, viewer-benefit framing

"Follow me for daily tips" ❌
"Follow me for systems that grow your business" ✅

## Script Writing Golden Rules

### ⚡ RULE 1 — Keep it 6th Grade Level
**Readability Score Target:** 5th–6th grade

**Why:** Simplifying complex ideas = mastery

**Tools:** Grammarly, Hemingway App, readability-score.com

---

### ✓ RULE 2 — Value Per Minute
**Cross out every unnecessary sentence**

**Process:**
1. Write draft
2. Cross out sentence
3. Re-read
4. Still makes sense? → sentence was unnecessary

**Result:** Dense value = more rewatches
