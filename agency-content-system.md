## Core Philosophy

### Principles

## Content Funnel (3 Layers)

### 🔴 Top — Awareness (Short-form virality)
**Goal:** Get eyeballs
- Educational, broad topics
- 5–10x average views rule
- Never try to sell in short-form

### 🟡 Middle — Trust (Long-form depth)
**Goal:** Build loyalty
- 30-60 min value bombs
- Client onboardings live
- In-person events

### 🟢 Bottom — Conversion (Proof overwhelm)
**Goal:** Generate sales
- Instagram Story sequences
- Client interviews (podcast-style)
- Social proof barrage

## Hook System (8 Types)

### 1-4 Text-Based Hooks
- **Contrarian**: "Most people think X. That's why they fail."
- **Specific Number**: "9 out of 10 founders..."
- **Direct Statement**: "You don't have a content problem..."
- **Curiosity Gap**: "Here's what nobody tells you..."

### 5-8 Visual Hooks
- **Story Open**: Narrative tension
- **Before/After**: Transformation showcase
- **Framework Reveal**: "The 3-pillar content system..."
- **Silent/Visual**: Pure visual storytelling
